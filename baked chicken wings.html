Baked Chicken Wings

Prep Time 20 minutes
Cook Time 30 minutes
Serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 1 kg of chicken wings
  • Olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic cloves: 4, minced
  • Dried oregano: 1 tsp
  • Sweet paprika: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt & pepper: To taste

Instructions

Marinate

  1. Combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, paprika, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add chicken wings and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Let sit for at least 20 minutes (up to 24 hours) in the fridge for best flavor.

Prepare

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper for easy cleanup.
  3. Pat marinated chicken wings dry with paper towels to ensure crispiness.

Bake

  1. Arrange chicken wings in a single layer on the prepared tray.
  2. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Optional: Add roasted vegetables to the tray halfway through baking.

Rest & Serve

  1. Allow wings to rest for 5 minutes before serving—this keeps them juicy.
  2. Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon wedges if desired.
  3. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or as a standalone main.
