Chicken Skewers

Prep Time 25 minutes
Cook Time 10-12 minutes
Serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast: 1 kg, cut into 3cm cubes
  • Olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic cloves: 4, minced
  • Dried oregano: 2 tsp
  • Fresh rosemary: 1 tsp, chopped
  • Sweet paprika: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt & pepper: To taste
  • Vegetables (optional): Zucchini slices, red onion chunks, bell peppers
  • Wooden skewers: Soaked in water

Instructions

Marinate

  1. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, rosemary, paprika, salt and pepper.
  2. Add chicken cubes and toss to coat evenly.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 20 minutes (up to 24 hours for deeper flavor).

Prep Skewers

  1. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning.
  2. Pat chicken dry before threading onto skewers.

Assemble

  1. Thread marinated chicken and vegetables onto skewers, alternating as desired.
  2. Leave small gaps between ingredients to allow heat to circulate evenly.

Grill

  1. Heat grill or griddle pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Place skewers on grill and cook for 8-10 minutes total.
  3. Turn every 2-3 minutes for even cooking.
  4. Check that internal temperature reaches 75°C (167°F).

Rest & Serve

  1. Allow skewers to rest for 5 minutes before serving to retain juiciness.
  2. Serve hot with fresh lemon wedges and your favorite sides.
