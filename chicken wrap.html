Grilled Chicken Wrap

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 10-12 minutes
Serves 2 wraps

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • Chicken breast: 300g, pounded thin
  • Olive oil: 1 tbsp
  • Garlic powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Paprika: 1/2 tsp
  • Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
  • Salt & pepper: To taste

For the Wraps

  • Flour tortillas: 2 large (10-inch)
  • Hummus or mayo: 2 tbsp
  • Fresh lettuce: A handful (romaine or mixed greens)
  • Tomatoes: 1 medium, sliced
  • Cucumber: 1/2, sliced into sticks
  • Red bell pepper: 1/2, sliced into strips
  • Red onion: 1/4, thinly sliced
  • Feta cheese or cheddar: 50g, crumbled or sliced
  • Fresh herbs: Cilantro or parsley, chopped

Instructions

Prepare the Chicken

  1. Place chicken breast on a cutting board and pound with a meat mallet until about 1/2 inch thick.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the chicken breast.
  4. Let sit for 5-10 minutes to marinate.

Cook the Chicken

  1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook marinated chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature 75°C/167°F).
  3. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 2 minutes.
  4. Slice the cooked chicken into strips.

Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Wash and slice all fresh vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Pat dry to prevent the wraps from becoming soggy.
  3. Chop fresh herbs and set aside.

Assemble the Wraps

  1. Warm tortillas in a skillet for 30 seconds on each side to make them more pliable, or wrap in a damp towel to soften.
  2. Lay a tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of hummus or mayo down the center of the tortilla.
  4. Layer lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and bell pepper on top.
  5. Add the sliced grilled chicken on top of the vegetables.
  6. Sprinkle with red onion, cheese, and fresh herbs.

Wrap & Serve

  1. Fold one side of the tortilla over the filling.
  2. Fold the bottom edge up and tightly roll the wrap away from you, keeping the filling tucked in.
  3. Slice diagonally if desired for easier handling and better presentation.
  4. Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce (ranch, sriracha mayo, or hummus).
