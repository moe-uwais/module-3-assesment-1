Grilled Salmon

Prep Time 15 minutes
Cook Time 8-12 minutes
Serves 4 people

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets: 4 skin-on fillets (6 oz each)
  • Olive oil: 1/4 cup
  • Lemon juice: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic cloves: 4, minced
  • Dried oregano: 2 tsp
  • Fresh rosemary: 1 tsp, chopped
  • Sweet paprika: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt & pepper: To taste
  • Lemon slices: For serving

Instructions

Prepare the Marinade

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together olive oil and lemon juice until combined.
  2. Stir in minced garlic, oregano, rosemary, paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon in a shallow dish and pour marinade over it, coating evenly.

Marinate

  1. Let salmon marinate for at least 15 minutes in the refrigerator.
  2. Avoid over-marinating—too long may "cook" the fish with the citrus juice.

Grill

  1. PREHEAT: Heat grill or griddle pan to medium-high heat.
  2. REMOVE: Take salmon from marinade and pat dry gently.
  3. Place salmon skin-side up on grill and cook for 4-6 minutes per side.
  4. Fish is done when it flakes easily with a fork and reaches 63°C (145°F) internal temperature.

Rest & Serve

  1. Allow salmon to rest for 2-3 minutes on a warm plate.
  2. Serve with fresh lemon slices and your favorite side dishes.
  3. Optional: Drizzle with remaining marinade if desired.
