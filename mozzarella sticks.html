Crispy Mozzarella Sticks

Prep Time 20 minutes + 2 hours freezing
Cook Time 8-10 minutes
Serves 4 people (12 sticks)

Ingredients

For the Mozzarella Sticks

  • Mozzarella cheese string: 12 sticks (about 300g)
  • All-purpose flour: 100g
  • Eggs: 2, beaten
  • Breadcrumbs (panko): 150g
  • Italian seasoning: 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder: 1/2 tsp
  • Salt & pepper: To taste
  • Oil for frying: Vegetable or canola oil (about 2 cups)

For Serving

  • Marinara sauce: 200ml, for dipping
  • Ranch dressing: Optional alternative dipping sauce
  • Fresh parsley: For garnish

Instructions

Prepare the Coating Station

  1. Set up three shallow bowls for breading: flour in the first, beaten eggs in the second, and panko breadcrumbs mixed with Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper in the third.
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.

Coat the Mozzarella Sticks

  1. Working with one stick at a time, roll it in the flour until fully coated. Shake off excess flour.
  2. Dip the floured stick into the beaten egg, coating all sides.
  3. Roll in the panko breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently so the coating adheres.
  4. Place on the prepared baking tray.
  5. Repeat with remaining mozzarella sticks.

Double Coat (Optional but Recommended)

  1. For extra crispiness, repeat the egg and breadcrumb coating process a second time on each stick.
  2. This prevents the cheese from leaking during frying.

Freeze

  1. Place the baking tray in the freezer for at least 2 hours, or overnight if possible.
  2. Freezing is crucial—it prevents the cheese from leaking out during frying.

Fry the Mozzarella Sticks

  1. Heat oil in a deep pot or deep fryer to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Carefully place frozen mozzarella sticks into the hot oil (work in small batches to avoid overcrowding).
  3. Fry for 30-45 seconds on each side until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to remove and place on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  5. Let cool for 1-2 minutes before serving to prevent burning your mouth on the hot cheese.

Serve

  1. Arrange mozzarella sticks on a serving plate.
  2. Pour warm marinara sauce into a small dipping bowl and place alongside.
  3. Garnish with fresh parsley for color and freshness.
  4. Serve immediately while hot and crispy.
